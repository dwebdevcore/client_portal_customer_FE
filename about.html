About Us

The new standard in cannabis technology.

NUGL is the world’s first cannabis search app built for the people, by the people. Our goal is to build the most user-friendly app experience in the cannabis industry by listening to our users and giving them what they want. NUGL is the only cannabis search app that offers equal and unbiased search results. We don’t sell top-spot listings or fake reviews, so our data stays true. Use NUGL to search for genuine user-rated dispensaries, strains, doctors, lawyers, cannabis service providers, vape shops, hydro stores, brands and more.

Join the revolution!

We love ALL user suggestions. Help us bring you the best experience we can by telling us what you want to see in future releases. Together, we can build the new standard in cannabis technology!

Members of the Board

John R. Armstrong - Board Member

John R. Armstrong is a founding partner of Horwitz + Armstrong, A Professional Law Corporation, a full service general business firm handling all aspects of litigation and business strategy and advice. Mr. Armstrong and his Partner, Lawrence Horwitz each have over 10 years’ experience in the business cannabis space, and have represented cannabis professionals in all aspects of business, from business formation, license and estate acquisitions, corporate mergers and acquisitions, capital raises and alternative financing, contracts, and dispute resolution.

Previously, Mr. Armstrong was a partner at Murtaugh Meyer Nelson & Treglia LLP, handling general business litigation and trials, arbitrations, and mediations in various fields including construction, insurance, trade secrets, intellectual property, wage and hour complaints, employment issues, corporate dissolution disputes, probate disputes, and other general business disputes.

Mr. Armstrong graduated cum laude from Creighton University School of Law in 1996 where he was the Lead Articles Editor for the Creighton Law Review and a member of the environmental moot court team. He earned undergraduate degrees in English and Political Science from California State University Fullerton in 1993, where he was a member of the speech and debate team.

Mr. Armstrong has been admitted to the California State Bar since 1996, and is admitted to the North, Eastern, Central, and Southern District Courts for California, the U.S. Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, the Federal Circuit Court of Appeals, the U.S. Court of International Trade, the U.S. Court of Federal Claims, and to the United States Supreme Court. Mr. Armstrong is also member of the prestigious Million Dollar Advocates Forum, limited to lead trial counsel who have successfully tried a case resulting in a jury verdict of $1,000,000 or more. Mr. Armstrong was also a contributing editor to the Ninth Edition of Black’s Law Dictionary, and has been a member of the California State Bar’s Sub-Committee on the official California Civil Jury Instructions (CACI).

General Council

Horwitz + Armstrong

Horwitz + Armstrong, A Professional Law Corporation, represents businesses operating primarily in Southern California but also throughout the world. Our firm handles the myriad of legal issues that arise from business operations, including entity formation, intellectual property protection, raising of capital, mergers and acquisitions, drafting contracts and associated advice, dissolution and bankruptcy. Our trial lawyers represent business interests in a broad variety of disputes including contract disputes, shareholder and partnership disagreements, intellectual property issues, collection matters and virtually any other dispute arising from business operations. Our clients range from small, privately held local business to mid-size technology companies with operations throughout the world.

Our practice does not focus upon any one industry, but instead our clients come from virtually every economic sector including financial management and planning, manufacturing and distribution, professional and medical services, energy and alternative energy, high technology and software. Our firm has a unique expertise in assisting companies in identifying and raising financial capital in both the private and public markets. We focus upon supporting business operations and assuring that, if at all possible, operational requirements take priority over legal issues. We pride ourselves on our business acumen and experience, rather than simply providing legal advice. We also pride ourselves at providing high quality but cost effective litigation services when informal conflict resolution efforts fail.
